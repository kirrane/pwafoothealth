About Us

Foot Health Podiatry Clinic was established by Karen Fahy in 2002. All our Podiatrists hold a minimum qualification of a Bachelor of Science degree in Podiatric Medicine.

At Foot Health Podiatry Clinic we provide expert care, diagnosis and treatment of ankle and foot disorders for children and adults. As Podiatrists we are specifically trained clinical physicians whose training allows us to diagnoses foot and ankle problems and provide a treatment plan tailored to the specific needs of each patient.
